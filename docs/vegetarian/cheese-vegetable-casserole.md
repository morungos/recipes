# Cheese and vegetable casserole

## Ingredients

* 120g cheese
* 3 eggs
* Milk
* 2 medium onions
* Other vegetables in proportion, e.g., carrots, swiss chard, etc.
* Salt, pepper, and nutmeg to season

## Method

Boil for 5 mins, then bake for ½ hour at 350°F (Regulo 4)
