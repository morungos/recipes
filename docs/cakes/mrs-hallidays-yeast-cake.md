# Mrs Halliday's yeast cake

> From my grandmother’s recipe book.

## Ingrediemts

* 1 lb flour
* ½ lb margarine
* ½ lb sultanas
* 1 oz yeast
* ½ lb sugar
* ½ lb currants
* 2 oz peel
* 1 tsp bicarbonate of soda

## Method

Crumble yeast into dry ingredients. 
Mix with water.
Put in well-greased tin.
Do not set to rise.
Place in moderate oven to bake.
