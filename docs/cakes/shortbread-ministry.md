# Shortbread (Ministry of Food version)

> From my grandmotherâ€™s recipe book.

## Ingredients

* 2 oz fat
* 4 oz flour
* 1 oz sugar
* A pinch of salt

## Method 

Cream fat well, cream in flour. Add sugar. 
Knead well and roll into a round. Prick. 
Bake in a moderate oven for 15 minutes.
