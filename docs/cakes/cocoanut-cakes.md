# Cocoanut cakes

> From my grandmotherâ€™s recipe book.

## Ingredients

* 4 oz flour
* 4 oz fine cocoanut
* 5 oz butter or margarine
* 5 oz sugar
* 2 eggs
* 1 tsp baking powder

## Method 

Cream butter and sugar. Add eggs and continue in usual way.
Make into little cakes in greased patty tins.
