# Treacle sponge

> From my grandmother's recipe book. 

## Ingredients

* 1/4 lb suet
* 3 tea cups of flour
* 1 dessert spoon of ground ginger
* 1 tsp bicarbonate of soda
* 1 tea cup of milk
* 1 tea cup syrup of treacle

## Method

Mix the dry ingredients, add syrup and milk and dissolved soda.
